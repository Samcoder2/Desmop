About Us

Desmop Multipurpose Investment is a fashion design company founded by Mrs Success; a 5-years plus experienced fashion designer.

She observed the stress and alarming cost people sacrifice at the feet of different tailors and fashion designers only to get an undesirable dress as outcome.

Unhappy about this, she saw it necessary to establish a cost-effective, detail-oriented and universal platform aimed not just at sewing dresses but also styling dresses for all and sundry.

And so she established Desmop Multipurpose Investment, a company devoted to ensuring that her customers wear the perfect dress fit for them.

So far, we have put smiles on people's faces and graced occasions with our well-tailored and well-crafted dresses and we will continue to do so till the whole world experience clothes at their best.
